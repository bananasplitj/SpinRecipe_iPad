Arrabbiata

This, Mario Batali wrote in 2013 in The Times, is one of his late-night favorites. Its uncomplicated nature lends itself to an after-midnight feast. It’s basically pasta with tomato sauce and cheese, but red pepper flakes give the sauce a delicious kick.

Ingredients

Instructions

  • 1. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water until tender.
  • 2. Heat olive oil in a skillet, add garlic and red pepper, and cook for 30 seconds.
  • 3. Add tomatoes and crush them with a wooden spoon.
  • 4. Simmer for 5-10 minutes and remove from heat.
  • 5. Mix the pasta with the sauce and serve with cheese and basil.
